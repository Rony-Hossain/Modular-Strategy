Build HybridScalp_v1 sequenced condition set
New file: HybridScalp.cs
A single IConditionSet that combines all layers in sequence. State machine: (1) Wait for CHoCH or BOS flag in SnapKeys. (2) Set directional bias and arm a zone-entry window (max 10 bars). (3) Within that window, check if price has retraced into an active FVG or OB zone (read ObBullActive/FvgBullActive from SnapKeys). (4) On retracement bar: require BarDelta aligned + AbsorptionScore > 30. (5) Require SupportResistanceResult shows no adverse zone within 0.3×ATR. (6) Emit entry at zone midpoint, stop below zone low (bull) or above zone high (bear), T1 = prior swing high (bull), T2 = 2×ATR. Score: 78 base + confluence bonuses up to 92. Window expires if price makes a new swing in the opposite direction.
